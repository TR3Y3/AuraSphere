# AuraSphere Weekly Sales Report — Format

The report has exactly these four sections, in this order:

---

**AURASPHERE WEEKLY SALES REPORT**

- **Rep / Owner:**
- **Organization:**
- **Week of:** (Monday date) – (Friday date)

## New Accounts Closed

Deals won this week (stage moved to a won stage), plus brand-new accounts
added.

| Company | Primary Contact | Amount | Notes |
|---------|-----------------|--------|-------|

## Existing Customers

Activity with current accounts this week (calls, meetings, emails logged,
issues resolved, upsells).

| Company | Activity | Owner | Notes |
|---------|----------|-------|-------|

## Pending Proposals

Open opportunities: deals in flight, quotes out, decision pending.

| Company | Contact | Stage | Amount | Next Step | Expected Close |
|---------|---------|-------|--------|-----------|----------------|

## Lost Business

Deals lost or accounts churned this week, with reason.

| Company | Amount | Reason | Salvageable? |
|---------|--------|--------|--------------|

---

> Column headers and the four section names/order above are the AuraSphere
> default. If the user confirms a different house format, update this file to
> match. Deal/amount columns populate once Phase 3 (deals) and Phase 4
> (activities) exist; before then, fill from new companies/contacts and
> user-supplied numbers.
