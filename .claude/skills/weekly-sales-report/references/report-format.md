# CorTrans Weekly Sales Report — Official Format

The report has exactly these four sections, in this order:

---

**CORTRANS WEEKLY SALES REPORT**

- **Rep:**
- **Week of:** (Monday date) – (Friday date)

## New Accounts Closed

Accounts that signed / shipped their first load with CorTrans this week.

| Company | Contact | Freight Type | Notes |
|---------|---------|--------------|-------|

## Existing Customers

Activity with current customers this week (loads moved, check-ins, issues
resolved, upsells).

| Company | Activity | Notes |
|---------|----------|-------|

## Pending Proposals

Open opportunities: quotes out, meetings held, decision pending.

| Company | Contact | Stage | Next Step | Expected Close |
|---------|---------|-------|-----------|----------------|

## Lost Business

Opportunities lost or customers churned this week, with reason.

| Company | Reason | Salvageable? |
|---------|--------|--------------|
